A B O U T



The Old Barber 90’s brings a high
standard of workmanship to gent’s grooming.



After cutting gent’s hair for many years in the general unisex
environment of hairdressing, Brian felt that some gents were
uneasy coming into a female dominated hair salon.



He wanted to create an equally high end environment where gents
could come and get an outstanding service and seek advice on
their grooming needs. A sanctuary for gents, a club if you like.
One where our patrons can relax in great surroundings while
experiencing the craft of a perfectly executed haircut using
techniques our barbers have perfected over years.
